What We Have To Offer

Brotherhood

Become part of a group of young men who share the same qualities and collegiate goals. With over 15,000 students, its easy to feel overwhelmed and out of place at Binghamton University. Joining a fraternity is a great way to build a core group of friends that last a lifetime.

Social Events

Not only does Phi Psi host social events throught the year, the brotherhood also mixes with sorority chapters at the University in order for young men and women of the Greek community to get to know each other

Scholastics

Phi Psi puts a strong emphasis on academics. We offer free tutoring to all active brothers who achieve academic standards set forth by the chapter. Each year hundreds of thousands of dollars are available in the form of national scholarships to assist in offsetting that ever rising cost of a college education.

Philanthropy/University Involvement

At any given time, you can find a number of Phi Psi working or exercising at Fitspace (the campus gym). As members of the IFC, we have a large network of Greek organizations that work together to host events communally. Our philanthropic endeavours like the Steven Kovacs Walk Your Heart Out Color Run and our participation with the Boys & Girls Club allow us to give back to the community.

Leadership

Each spring, select members of Phi Psi chapters are invited to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico to take part in the American Leadership Academy hosted by Brother Jerry Nelson (Founder Of Ticketmaster). The event brings successful Phi Psi Alumni from around the world to discuss leadership principles, character development and overall fraternity management.

Alumni Network

As you move from college to the working world, who you know is just as important as what you know. Phi Psi alumni from across the nation are eager to bring aboard graduates of their fraternity. A few famous Phi Psis include Tony Horton (P90x), Zach Braff(Scrubs), Jerry Nelson (Ticketmaster) and former NY Mayor, Michael Bloomberg.
