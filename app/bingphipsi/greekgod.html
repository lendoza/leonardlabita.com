AePHi Greek God Competition

Each year on Alumni Weekend, Binghamton Universities Alpha Epsilon Phi Sorority hosts the Greek God Competition. The competition puts Binghamton's top fraternities against each other in a contest of bodybuilding, skit performance, and philantropic prowess. On October 18th 2014, the 29th Annual Greek God competition will take place to raise money for the Elizabeth Glaser Pediatric AIDS Foundation and Sharsheret For Womens Breast Cancer. Anthony Leighton will be representing us this year and looks to improve on our third place finish from last year.
