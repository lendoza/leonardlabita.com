The Steven Kovacs Walk Your Heart Out Memorial


The Steven Kovacs Walk Your Heart Out Memorial was first created in 2009 in response to the sudden tragic passing of our brother Steven Kovacs. Steve was an outstanding student at Binghamton University who loved to give back to the community.

On October 19th 2014 , the brothers of Phi Kappa Psi will be hosting the 5th Annual Steven Kovacs Walk Your Heart Out Color Run/5k. There will also be food and awesome raffles during the event, which will be raffled off during the day of the event. We encourage you to come and support our cause and together we can make a difference. Since it will be a color run, we recommend dressing appropriately! We will be supplying a limited amount of complimentary T-shirts and wristbands, so be sure to get there early.

We strongly encourage you to register in groups of 5 or 10, as the registration fee is $6/person for groups of 10+, $8/person for groups of 5+, and $10/person for individual participants. Prizes will be awarded for both the fastest individual times and fastest times per team. Have an uneven amount of runners? That’s fine; you’re top five participants scores will be used to total your score.

- 12:00-1:00 pm- Day of Race Registration
- 1:15pm – Memorial Lap
- 1:45pm – Color Run (Intramural field behind East Gym and through nature preserve)

  • Parking will be available in the East Gym
  • Food and drinks will be available
  • Bring blankets, footballs, etc to relax on the field during the event
  • If you have any questions, feel free to contact James O'Donnell (jodonne3@binghamton.edu)
