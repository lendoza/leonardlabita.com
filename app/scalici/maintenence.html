Year Round Maintenence

As the seasons change, maintaining your property can become a hassle. Snow, leaves, grass, and more become difficult to manage. Luckily at Scalici Farm & Greenhouses we offer a variety of maintenance service throughout the year for your convenience.

Our team of landscape architects provide:

  • Bed Mulching
  • Snow Removal
  • Leaf Removal
  • Grass Cutting
  • Shrub Trimming
  • Fertilizing

If you would like to schedule a consultation or have more questions about the maintenence process, please feel free to visit us at the farm: 634 Colts Neck Road Freehold, NJ or contact us at 1-732-863-9022
