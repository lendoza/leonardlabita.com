Tree Services

Unsightly tree and stumps can get in the way of an appealing landscape. We at Scalici Farm & Greenhouses understand this and thus offer multiple tree services for your needs.

Our tree services include:

  • Tree Removal
  • Trimming
  • Prunning
  • Stump Grinding/Removal
  • Emergency Clean Up

Due to the nature of tree removal and modification that is on government property, we highly recommend that you contact us to get the job done. We will work with you and within the limitations of the law to see that the project is done correctly.

If you would like to schedule a consultation or have more questions about the tree service process, please feel free to visit us at the farm: 634 Colts Neck Road Freehold, NJ or contact us at 1-732-863-9022
